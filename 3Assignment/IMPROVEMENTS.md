## 🔧 **How to Use New Features**

### Pharmacy Shopping
1. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
2. See your cart update in real-time
3. Click "Clear Cart" to reset
4. Click "Proceed to Checkout" to complete order

### Book Appointment
1. Select a doctor from dropdown
2. Choose your preferred date
3. Pick a time slot
4. Add reason for visit (optional)
5. Click "Confirm Appointment"

### Calculate BMI
1. Enter your height in cm
2. Enter your weight in kg
3. Click "Calculate BMI"
4. Get personalized health advice

### View Fitness
- Check your weekly calories burned chart
- View fitness statistics on the right panel
- Monitor your health metrics at a glance
